∫ Equations & Definitions

Doppler Equations
Wave: v = f · λ
Ahead: fo = fs · (v + vo) ÷ (v − vs)
Behind: fo = fs · (v − vo) ÷ (v + vs)
λ: λ = v ÷ f
Variable Definitions
fssource frequency (Hz)
foobserved frequency (Hz)
vvelocity of sound waves = 343 m/s
vovelocity of observer (m/s) — 0, observers are stationary
vsvelocity of source (m/s)
λwavelength (m)
Live Calculations
